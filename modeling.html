FAQ

Common Questions



What should I send to start?+

Sketches/photos with key dimensions, 2D drawings (PDF/DWG), existing meshes (STL/OBJ/3MF) for cleanup, or a reference part for reverse-engineering.

Which file formats do you deliver?+

Print-ready: STL or 3MF (mm). CAD: STEP/IGES. Visual: OBJ/FBX/GLB with optional UVs/textures. Exploded views and 2D drawings available on request.

How long does a typical model take?+

Simple parts: 24–48 h. Medium assemblies/organic shapes: 2–5 days. Complex reverse-engineering: 5–10+ days, depending on detail and revisions.

Will you make it print-ready (DFM)?+

Yes. We check wall thickness, overhangs, tolerances, hole compensation/threads, and ensure watertight, manifold geometry for clean slicing.

Do you sign NDAs and who owns the IP?+

Yes, we can sign your NDA. After payment per agreement, all deliverables and IP belong to you. Your files remain private and confidential.
