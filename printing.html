Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions



What format should I upload?+

STL is preferred (mm). OBJ/STEP work too. Make sure models are watertight with correct scale.

How accurate are parts?+

FDM ±0.2 mm or ±0.2% (greater of the two). Resin ±0.05 mm typical.

Do you sign NDAs?+

Yes. Your files stay private; we can sign an NDA upon request.
