Our Story

From hobby bench to full-service 3D studio

DNP 3D began with a single desktop printer and a passion for turning ideas into real parts. We now support makers, students, SMEs, and product teams with clean CAD, reliable printing, and thoughtful finishing. We obsess over tolerance stacks, clean topology, and practical DFM so your parts look great and assemble right—first time. Whether you bring a napkin sketch, a messy STL, or a full STEP file, we choose the shortest path from brief to part.

What drives us

Principles we build into every model & print


Clean, watertight CAD

No non-manifold edges, proper normals, and smart topology for fast slicing & revisions.

DFM for FDM & Resin

Wall thickness, overhangs, tolerances, and hole compensation checked before print.

Reliable QA

We validate scale, fit, and finish—so assemblies click together the way you expect.

Fast turnaround

Simple parts in 24–48h. Complex organic or reverse-engineering within agreed timelines.

Friendly support

Share sketches, photos, or parts—we’ll guide you to the cleanest, cost-effective result.

Clear deliverables

STEP/IGES for CAD, STL/3MF for print, optional OBJ/FBX/GLB, plus drawings on request.

How we work

Simple, collaborative, predictable

1 — Brief

Send sketches/photos or meshes with key dimensions. We confirm scope & quote.

2 — Concept & constraints

Materials, tolerances, and process limits set early to avoid rework.

3 — Modeling

Parametric or organic sculpting as needed; assemblies are checked for fit.

4 — Print & finish

FDM or resin, test slices for clean print, and post-processing for a neat look.

5 — Delivery

Files + parts delivered. Revisions supported, and we can sign NDAs on request.
